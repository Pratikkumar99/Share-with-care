Step 1: Restaurants & Hotels List Surplus Food

Food providers register on our platform and list surplus food available for donation or minimal cost.

Step 2: NGOs Browse & Order

NGOs browse available food items and place requests for donation or purchase at minimal cost.

Step 3: Food Pickup & Delivery

Once approved, NGOs coordinate pickup or delivery to ensure timely distribution to those in need.

Step 4: Quality Check

We ensure that the donated food meets hygiene and safety standards before distribution.

Step 5: Meal Distribution

NGOs distribute the food to the needy, ensuring that it reaches the right beneficiaries.

Step 6: Impact Tracking

We track food donations and provide impact reports to ensure transparency and efficiency.
